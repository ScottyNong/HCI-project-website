US Drug Consumption - Socioeconomic Analysis

Research Question

How do socioeconomic factors such as income level, education, and employment status influence patterns of drug use in different regions?

What does the visualization show?

This interactive map visualizes drug consumption patterns across the United States, correlating them with key socioeconomic factors like income, education, and employment.

Who is this for?

This visualization is designed for students, young people and the general public interested in understanding the broader context of drug use.

Why should we care?

Understanding the socioeconomic determinants of drug consumption can help policymakers design more effective intervention strategies.

How to read the visualization?

Each state is color-coded based on drug consumption levels. Additional overlays show correlations with income, education, and employment. Users can interact with the map to explore detailed statistics.
